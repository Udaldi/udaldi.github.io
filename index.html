Tell me about the illustrations and photography.

I've been a graphic novelist since I was a kid. I always drew in my life. Experimented different materials for different expression... It is unbelievable that drawing can give people emotions, isn't it?
Sometimes we take this for granted and we simply forget about it, but it's really amazing, almost a magic.
As a child, I saw the graphic novel as my way to tell stories. Still I think it's amazing that only pen and papers can do so much!!
